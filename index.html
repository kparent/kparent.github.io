RecCheck

RecCheck is an iOS application built for sports lovers who want to get out there and play. This app allows the user to organize or find local pickup games. RecCheck can serve as central location where your group can check for upcoming games, or as a source to make new friends through the power of pickup games.

RecCheck was my first foray into mobile development during my senior year at UCSC, and has been an incredible learning experience. In the interest of focusing development on the app's core, I employed Parse, an handy Backend as a Service platform built on top of Postgres to abstract data storage and access. I wrote the application entirely in Objective-C, and have employed many cool design patterns learned along the way such as MVC, callbacks, blocks, delegates, and observers.

The beta is coming October 2015, so visit the website to find out more about the project and shoot me an email to get your beta download!
